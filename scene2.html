Netflix Originals Quality & Quantity: What is the rate of increase of 'good' Netflix Originals year over year?

  • From the previous scene, you might have observed that Netflix Documentaries on average have a higher IMDB Score than Dramas or Comedies, among other genres.
  • What behavior could be driving the high IMDB score and count of Documentaries? Could there be an impact from the covid era in 2020 on high quality Netflix Originals?
  • This graph provides insights into those questions and more by showing the the change over time of 'good' Netflix Originals year over year. Good is defined as an IMDB score greater or equal to 7.0.
  • Since we saw that Documentaries typically have a higher IMDB score than dramas or comedies, the change in quantity of 'good' Netflix Originals between the current selected year and the prior selected year is annotated for those three genres.
  • Note that the size of the bubble indicates the number of 'good' productions in that genre for that year, and the color is aligned with each genre.
  • Try moving the slider to see how the productions in that genre accumulated over time. What do you notice about the rate of change of new dramas and comedies in later years compared to documentaries?
